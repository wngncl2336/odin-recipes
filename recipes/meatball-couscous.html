Meatball Couscous

Ingredients (per portion)

  • 3 meatballs
  • 1/2 cup of dried couscous
  • 120ml of chicken stock
  • 150-200ml of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 100g of spinach
  • 1/2Tbsp of cooking oil
  • Any amount of any cheese of your liking to garnish

Steps

  1. pour the olive oil into a frying pan and heat it on a stove to medium-high heat.
  2. put the meatballs on the heated pan and let it cook.

    3. whilst waiting for the meatballs to cook prep the other ingredients

  3. dice the onion
  4. shred the spinach into small strips to garnish for later.
  5. put the chicken stock into a pot and bring it to a boil
  6. Additional step if you have stock cubes: boil water 120 ml of water and dissolve 1/4 a stock cube.

    7. Now that the meatballs are partially cooked:

  7. add in the diced onions and cook fully
  8. now that the meatballs and onions are mostly cooked add the tomato sauce and lower the heat to medium-low
  9. as the tomato sauce slowly simmers, put the dried couscous into a bowl and mix the hot stock into the grains
  10. take the meatballs off the heat and add the spinach now to let it wilt in the residual heat
  11. pour the meatball and sauce into the bowl of couscous and finish with an optional dash of cheese garnish
